About Me

I am a Lawyer and Researcher, currently a PhD candidate and Master in Law, State, and Constitution at the Graduate Program in Law at the University of Brasília (UnB). My research focuses on Transitional Justice, Human Rights, Social Movements, and Criminology in Latin America.

I have experience in research and academic work review. Currently, I serve as a UNESCO consultant at the Ministry of Justice and Public Security. I hold a Law degree from the Federal University of Santa Maria (2019).

Languages: Portuguese (Native), English (Fluent), Spanish (Fluent), French (Intermediate).
